Your day is all about you—we capture it through your guests’ eyes, so you can relive every laugh, tear, and moment instantly.

Get in touch

After several years of wedding planning, I am able to be at the right place at the right time, never missing the moments you want to relive the most. Films packed with all the real, raw feels and fun vibes. Delivered in 24 hours.

How it works

01
Save the date!

Make your booking with me via email or direct message and let me know what package you are interested in. Transfer the deposit, sign the contract, and just like that, your wedding content creator is booked!

02
Let’s chat!

One month before your wedding, we’ll have a video call to review what I’ll film and what each video & package includes. You can also share any must-have moments you want captured on your wedding day.

03
The big day!

On the day, I will come prepared with my equipment, any dress code you may have, and working smoothly alongside any other supplier you may have for your day! I cannot wait to capture the most special moments of your wedding day in a cinematic video ready-to-share.

04
Get Your Content!

Delivered in 24 hours.
You will receive a link to a google drive with an album of all unedited content of the day, and a folder of your polished ready-to-share videos.
